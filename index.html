Safety

Four layers of guardrails.

Nombo should be useful without becoming reckless. The safety design treats wallet autonomy as bounded, observable, and reversible.

Nombo rules

Max position size, max leverage, stop after losses, paper-first mode, no unsupported markets.

Wallet policy

Outflow caps, allowlists/blocklists, chain restrictions, signing policy, and approvals.

Venue limits

Margin, liquidation, order rules, deposit assets, and exchange-specific risk controls.

Funding cap

The simplest protection: only fund the agent wallet with what the user is prepared to risk.
