The landing pad for a normal user’s digital money life.

Nombo Alpha Desk is a guarded AI dashboard for wallets, chains, bridges, strategies, yield, records, taxes, and approvals. The user speaks in plain English; Nombo coordinates the CLIs, policies, and approval flows behind the scenes.

Core product thesis

Crypto is too fragmented for casual users. Alpha Desk becomes the traffic cop: read everything, explain everything, stage safe actions, and keep the human in control.

MetaMask Agent Wallet is the first connector. Future connectors can support other wallets, CLIs, chains, exchanges, tax tools, bridges, and KYC/payment rails without changing the user’s mental model.

Under construction
Future intake would ask what wallet/app the user has, what permissions are available, what signing model is required, and whether Nombo can read balances, stage transactions, or only provide guidance.
1. Select wallet type
MetaMask, Yoroi/Cardano, Solana, Bitcoin, exchange account, bridge/KYC rail, or custom CLI.
2. Connect read-only first
Start with visibility and explanations before any signing or value movement permission.
3. Map permissions to guardrails
Translate raw CLI/API permissions into user-friendly dials and policy labels.
4. Save connector profile
Add the wallet to Alpha Desk with records, approvals, limits, and Nombo rules attached.
Plain-English promise
“Tell Nombo what you want. Nombo figures out which wallet, chain, bridge, approval, and record entry is required.”
For novice users, this is the difference between crypto as a pile of apps and crypto as an understandable operating system.
